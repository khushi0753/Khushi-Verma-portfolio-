About Me

Hello! I’m Khushi Verma, a passionate and self-driven Frontend Developer with a strong enthusiasm for designing clean, modern, and responsive web interfaces. I have a solid grasp of core technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and C programming, along with foundational knowledge of Java. My passion for coding is fueled by a desire to bring creative ideas to life on the web and build seamless digital experiences that truly connect with users.

Over time, I’ve expanded my technical expertise by exploring and working with modern frameworks and libraries such as React.js, Bootstrap, and Tailwind CSS. These tools allow me to develop dynamic and scalable web applications that look great across all devices. I enjoy the challenge of taking static designs from tools like Figma and transforming them into fully interactive, pixel-perfect user interfaces.


I believe that the front-end of a website is more than just its appearance — it's about how users feel when they interact with it. That’s why I place a strong emphasis on user experience (UX), performance optimization, and accessibility in every project I work on. Beyond the technical aspects, I’m a quick learner, a great team player, and someone who always strives to stay updated with the latest trends in the ever-evolving web development world.


My mission is to build websites that are not only visually compelling but also intuitive, fast, and meaningful. I’m currently open to exciting opportunities where I can contribute, learn, and grow — all while building amazing digital experiences.
